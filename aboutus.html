Rendering the better surroundings in which students can secure their life along with the development of a more reliable nation is really a rigorous task. Advancing the same idea in April 2007, IIIT’S were created in March of 2008 under the chairmanship of Prof. K. C. Reddy. Nearly 6,500 high school graduates were admitted into a 6-year integrated B.Tech. program. This splendid idea also offers students to select their interested engineering branch after their completion of 2-years Pre-University Course. Going forward with this outstanding faculty were also recruited from the best educational institutions.

Department
Electronics and Communication Engineering

Along with the universities, an encouraging branch ECE was also started extending its roots by placing students in different companies with the best packages. It is really proud to say that ours is one of the 12 teams across the globe to be selected for the Grand Finale of the Global Design Contest – Innovate FPGA. Not only this, the projects “Smart Irrigation Based upon Crop Requirements”, “Project Green Globe” and “Scrap Collcetinf Self-Propelled Boat” stood in the top-30 finalists of the Swadeshi Microprocessor Challenge conducted by Meity. The department is also well equipped with labs which provide practical knowledge for the students to go through. It also facilitates research work and help students gain expertise in their interested domain. Many core companies like Intel, BEL, HPCL, Wipro, Analog Devices and Synopsis are placing the students of the department in their companies for their excellence and the count of internships and placements are increasing along with the packages. These are some of the milestones, there are and will be many more…
